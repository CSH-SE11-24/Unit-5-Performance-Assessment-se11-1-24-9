A Report Card On A NYC Subway System In Crisis

In recent years, the safety situation within the New York City subway system has regrettably taken a turn for the worse, evolving into a more perilous environment for commuters and residents alike. The escalating level of crime within the subway network has reached such alarming heights that traditional patrolling by New York City Police Officers has proven insufficient to maintain public safety. Consequently, the state-based military force known as the National Guard has been called upon to bolster security measures within the subway system. The current state of affairs has compelled New York's Governor, Kathy Hochul, to take decisive action by deploying 750 troops from the National Guard to assist in restoring order and safeguarding the well-being of subway riders.
